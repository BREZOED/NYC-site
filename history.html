History of NewYorkCoin

A timeline that shows NewYorkCoin History of the coin all the way to present day.

2014

  • NewYorkCoin’s blockchain began on March 6, 2014. To date, the NewYorkCoin community has not discovered the person or persons who implemented NewYorkCoin’s genesis block. For years, the NewYorkCoin blockchain has been supported by a decentralized assortment of loyal miners, even though there had not been any active development since NewYorkCoin’s genesis block.

2015

July

  • MrRex tries to revive the NewYorkCoin by creating the TronxRex github repo, updating the code to the 2015 standards. The revival doesn't work as intended

2017

Late 2017

  • In mid-to- late 2017, several miners and NewYorkCoin community members decided to commit resources to waking up this forgotten value transmission asset by building a development team and community to help NewYorkCoin realize its full potential as day-to- day permission-less global zero-fee payment solution.

2018

Januari

  • Early adopters begin taking steps to improve NewYorkCoin: Trading prices of NewYorkCoin hits $0.001 for the first time in its existence
  • NewYorkCoin trading volume reached $4.4 Million
  • NewYorkCoin market cap hits $145 Million that very same week

Januari

  • The NewYorkCoin community explodes: enthusiasts from all over the world coming together on Social Media's like Telegram and Discord are meeting and exchanging ideas
  • Some of these enthusiasts gather up and develoment team, marketing and social media team gets created.
  • The community decides the major action points for the coin and assembles a roadmap

Februari

  • Birth of the NewYorkCoin Community Website
  • First new release of the Core wallet: v1.1.2.2_beta

March

  • Testing with ElectrumX server integration starts. First transactions over the NewYorkCoin network are send succesfull!
  • Early miner Charles K. sets up the NewYorkCoin center in lower Manhattan to raise awareness about NewYorkCoin's existence, although many in the NewYorkCoin community cannot condone some of the center's content.

  • Another Release of the Core wallet, bringing better security, more modern design: v1.2.0.7_beta.
